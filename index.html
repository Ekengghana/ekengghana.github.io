About Us

Founded in 2020, is an African-owned holding company and the parent of Ekeng Technology—one of four subsidiaries under its umbrella. Over the years, the Group has established its footprint across the African continent, garnering strategic partnerships, cultivating long-term relationships, and securing the trust of clients both within and beyond Africa. Ekeng Technology is a future-focused technology company committed to harnessing digital innovation to address real-world challenges from clean energy to intelligent devices, our solutions are designed to elevate lifestyles, drive impact, and champion a greener planet. Rooted in purpose and powered by progress, Ekeng Technology is redefining what it means to be a modern African tech company—bold, sustainable, and built for the world..

Our work-and-pay system allows drivers to operate and gradually own their vehicles, reducing fuel costs and promoting economic empowerment at the lowest market rates!.

Our Mission

Empowering communities and leading Africa’s transition into a digitally connected yet an environmentally conscious future through smart devices, clean energy, and transformative tech solutions.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s most trusted name in innovative and transformative tech solutions with an environmental conscience.
