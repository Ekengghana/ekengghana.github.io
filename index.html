About Us

Ekeng Ghana Limited is a Ghana-based mobility solutions company accelerating the adoption of electric vehicles, focusing on BYD, and other sleek EV models for commercial drivers.

Our work-and-pay system allows drivers to operate and gradually own their vehicles, reducing fuel costs and promoting economic empowerment , all at a low cost!.

Our Mission

To make electric mobility accessible and affordable for commercial drivers in Ghana by providing structured EV ownership solutions.

Our Vision

To become Ghana’s leading electric mobility provider, driving sustainable transportation and empowering commercial drivers to transition into profitable, asset-owning entrepreneurs.

Our Solution

BYD Electric Vehicles

Durable, efficient, and sleek EVs for Ghana’s transport environment.

Sealion EV

Innovative EV solutions tailored for commercial use.

Work-and-Pay Model

Structured payments over time until full ownership is achieved.

Sustainable Transport

Reducing carbon emissions and promoting cleaner cities in Ghana.
