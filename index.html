About Us

Founded in 2020, is an African-owned holding company and the parent of Ekeng Technology—one of four subsidiaries under its umbrella. Over the years, the Group has established its footprint across the African continent, garnering strategic partnerships, cultivating long-term relationships, and securing the trust of clients both within and beyond Africa. Ekeng Technology is a future-focused technology company committed to harnessing digital innovation to address real-world challenges from clean energy to intelligent devices, our solutions are designed to elevate lifestyles, drive impact, and champion a greener planet. Rooted in purpose and powered by progress, Ekeng Technology is redefining what it means to be a modern African tech company—bold, sustainable, and built for the world. Lease-to-Own Program

Our lease-to-own contract is designed to keep commercial drivers on the driving seat of their earnings. With our lease-to-own contracts, driver have a fixed term of up to three years to complete the payment for the vehicle and payments are required weekly. This type of finance comes with comprehensive insurance, charging allowance and mandatory monthly service. Drivers are not required to make a deposit payment however; the option is open to drivers who wants to pay a deposit to help finish paying for the vehicle earlier.
